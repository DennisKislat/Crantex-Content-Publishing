Privacy Policy

Acme Content Bot — Content Posting Application

Last updated: April 14, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Acme Content Bot ("we", "our", "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application to publish content to TikTok. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our content publishing service:

  • TikTok Account Data: When you authorize our App, we receive your TikTok username, display name, and avatar via the TikTok API. We use this solely to identify your account for posting.
  • Content Data: Videos, images, captions, and hashtags you submit for posting. This data is forwarded directly to TikTok via the Content Posting API and is not stored on our servers beyond the time needed to complete the upload.
  • Authorization Tokens: OAuth access tokens required to post content on your behalf. Tokens are stored securely and encrypted at rest.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To publish content to your TikTok account as directed by you
  • To verify your account identity and authorization status
  • To communicate errors or issues with content posting

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the content posting service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • TikTok: Content and posting instructions are sent to TikTok as required by the Content Posting API.
  • Service Providers: We may use cloud infrastructure providers to host and run the application. These providers are bound by data processing agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Data Retention

  • Content files: Deleted from our systems within 24 hours of successful posting.
  • Access tokens: Retained until you revoke authorization or delete your account.
  • Posting logs: Retained for 90 days for debugging, then permanently deleted.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) for all API communications
  • Encryption at rest for stored tokens and credentials
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security reviews and dependency updates

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke our access to your TikTok account at any time via your TikTok settings
  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you
  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
